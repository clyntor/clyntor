About Me

I'm the Lead Developer of Aegisum — a cryptocurrency project focused on scam prevention and education. I build and maintain the main sourcecode of the blockchain, along with the website, web wallet, mining pool, and more.


I first started my crypto journey back in 2015. I wasn't exactly great at the trading side — truthfully, the only consistent part was losing money. But over time, my interest shifted from charts to tech. In recent years, I've become increasingly focused on blockchain fundamentals and, more recently, web development. Merging these two worlds is a challenge I take seriously — and it's what drives most of my work today.

Projects

Aegisum Core

A fully decentralized, layer 1 cryptocurrency coin focused on scam prevention and education. Founded in February 2025, we are building tools to educate users about scams and to identify rug pulls.

Aegisum Web Wallet

Secure web wallet for Aegisum Coins, featuring:

  • Instantaneous transactions
  • Free faucet claims every hour
  • Voucher/gift system
  • And more!

Aegisum's Open Source Block Explorer

Our open source blockchain explorer. Installable in less than 10 minutes, I made the commands easy to copy in the docs. All you need is Aegisum Core running and you can get started.

Aegisum Mining Pool

Official Aegisum mining pool with 0% fees and no registration required. Features automatic hourly payouts, proportional block distribution, and comprehensive mining statistics. Simply use your wallet address as the username and start mining immediately.

Aegisum Faucet

A dedicated faucet for free Aegisum coins with tiered payouts based on faucet balance. Perfect for testing and development, users can claim free AEGS tokens every hour. Features a clean interface with automatic payout adjustments and anti-abuse features.

Aegisum Website

The main website and landing page of Aegisum. With over 11,000 unique visitors monthly, the website sees significant traffic. We host the education centre there, and it's the hub of everything Aegisum.
